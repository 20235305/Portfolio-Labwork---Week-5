Paolo Jr. Costarelli has 13 years of dance experience and his passion is to study different styles . From salsa , bachata , contemporary , ballet & hip hop. Paolo is a Dancer , choreographer and a teacher for competitions , social dancing and congresses. He has had the opportunity to perform in the most prestigious European congresses and he also fortunately had the chance to expand his knowledge with the greatest salsa teachers out there . His specialty is Salsa, in which he has his own style that unifies the mambo, cuban salsa, rumba and guaguanco. With 11 years of competitions behind him - Paolo won 7 Italian titles, 1 European title and 1 World Championship title. He is perpetually seeking new stimulation - to improve and have fun, and he is ready to share his knowledge at the Trinity Dance School. What an addition to the team - welcome on board
